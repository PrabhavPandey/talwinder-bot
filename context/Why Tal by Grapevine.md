Come for the tool, stay for the network. 

A lot of effort has gone into creating all the ideas around ‘making hiring better’ that we’ve made.

Round1 leveraged AI to understand a candidate better; but the agency business at the back of it is great for revenue but not for scale (yet) 

NextBoss is a long running thesis, it approaches change ‘bottom up’ by trying to connect hiring managers who will hire by themselves with candidates who are looking for a job.

Tal is an approach to make a fun, extremely intelligent, high utility, ‘give’ product to jobseekers in India that keeps them entertained while helping them find the job of their lives.

But we realize two things: 
- Each one of these products / businesses are incredibly difficult to build standalone
- The tough part is the hiring manager/business/boss side which always sees more friction 
- That side will always see friction unless we have enough candidates on our platform 
- We will never have enough candidates on a platform if as long as our approach to them and way of getting them is divided!

Tal by Grapevine combines all these: 
- Tal serves as the onboarding and ongoing engagement layer - helping a jobseeker find jobs whether or not we have them yet. It ensures delightful onboarding and ongoing value as a single player game.
- Round1 is the layer that helps understand a user more deeply - talking to them, understanding who they are what they are good at - information that is gold in us being able to match them (at the same time - we will give them feedback on how they talk and how they sound) 
- NextBoss is the ultimate play - helping them finally do the deed - meet the hiring manager, directly, and get the real job. 

While the story ties in super well together… what’s the journey of a user on the Boss App? 

1. He enters and lands on a chat with Tal who talks to him, makes his profile, and tells him everything that is about to happen
2. He can take a Round1 if he’s a serious jobseeker - to see if he can be a part of ‘Tal's Den’ 
3. He sees a feed ‘yet to load’ which shows that when he becomes eligible, hiring managers will start appearing right here on this screen.
